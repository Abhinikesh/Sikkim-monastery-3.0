Travel Tips

Essential information for visiting Sikkim's monasteries and cultural sites

Entry Requirements

Indian nationals need no special permits, but foreign nationals require an Inner Line Permit (ILP) which can be obtained online or at entry points.

Best Time to Visit

March to June and September to November offer the best weather. Monsoon season (July-August) can be challenging due to landslides.

What to Wear

Dress modestly when visiting monasteries. Cover your shoulders and knees. Remove shoes and hats before entering prayer halls.

Photography

Ask permission before taking photos inside monasteries. Some areas may prohibit photography. Respect the sacred nature of these spaces.

Transportation

Hire local taxis or use shared jeeps for monastery visits. Roads can be steep and winding. Consider motion sickness medication.

Health & Safety

Acclimatize to high altitude gradually. Carry basic medications. Stay hydrated and avoid alcohol at high altitudes.
