Local Cuisine

Discover authentic Sikkimese flavors and traditional monastery meals

Momos

Steamed dumplings filled with vegetables or meat, served with spicy chutney. A staple street food in Sikkim.

Thukpa

Hearty noodle soup with vegetables and meat, perfect for the cold mountain climate of Sikkim.

Gundruk

Fermented leafy green vegetable dish, a traditional Nepali preparation popular in Sikkim.

Sinki

Fermented radish taproot, a traditional pickle that adds tangy flavor to Sikkimese meals.

Chhurpi

Hard cheese made from yak milk, often used in traditional Sikkimese and Tibetan dishes.

Tongba

Traditional fermented millet beer served in wooden containers, a cultural drink of the region.
