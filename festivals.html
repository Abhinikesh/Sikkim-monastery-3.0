Festivals & Events

Experience the vibrant cultural celebrations of Sikkim throughout the year

Losar Festival

Tibetan New Year celebration with traditional dances, butter sculptures, and monastery ceremonies across Sikkim. Experience the vibrant colors, music, and spiritual rituals.

Buddha Purnima

Celebrating the birth of Buddha with spiritual gatherings, rituals, and prayers at all major monasteries. Join thousands of devotees in this sacred celebration.

Durga Puja

Major Hindu festival marking victory of good over evil. Experience elaborate puja ceremonies, cultural performances, and community gatherings throughout Sikkim.

Losoong/Namsoong

Saga Dawa

Buddhist festival celebrating the birth, enlightenment, and death of Buddha. Join processions, prayers, and spiritual activities at monasteries.
